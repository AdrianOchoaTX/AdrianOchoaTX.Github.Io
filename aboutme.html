About Me

Hello, I am Adrian Ochoa, a recent graduate with a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering.
As a developer with a strong passion for technology and developing interactive solutions, I am focused on expanding my knowledge and skills in areas like C#, .NET, and Unity. I have been working on various projects that allow me to explore different aspects of development, from building web applications using ASP.NET Core and deploying them to the cloud using Azure, to creating engaging games in Unity. I am driven by a desire to solve problems and create efficient, scalable solutions. My journey so far has taught me the importance of continuous learning and adaptability, which I apply by keeping up with the latest industry trends and technologies. I am eager to collaborate on challenging projects that push the boundaries of my abilities and contribute to meaningful outcomes. In the future, I aim to specialize in full-stack development, mastering both front-end and back-end technologies, while also delving deeper into cloud services and game development. I am excited about the endless possibilities in the tech world and am committed to becoming a proficient developer who can bring innovative ideas to life.
